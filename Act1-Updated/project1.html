Unliwings

Unliwings is a food delivery app that offers unlimited wings for a fixed price. The app allows users to customize their orders, track their deliveries, and enjoy a seamless ordering experience.

Project Details

Unliwings was designed to provide a unique and enjoyable food delivery experience. The app features a user-friendly interface, easy navigation, and a variety of customization options for users to choose from. The goal of the project was to create an app that not only meets the needs of customers but also provides a fun and engaging way to order food.
