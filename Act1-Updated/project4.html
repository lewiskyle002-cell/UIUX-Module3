The Fall

The Fall is a mobile application designed to help users track and prevent falls. The app uses sensors to detect falls and provides real-time alerts to caregivers or emergency contacts. It also offers features such as fall history tracking, personalized safety tips, and emergency response options.

Project Details

The Fall project was developed with the aim of improving the safety and well-being of individuals at risk of falling. The app was designed to be user-friendly and accessible, with a focus on providing timely assistance in the event of a fall. The project involved extensive research and testing to ensure that the app effectively meets the needs of users and enhances their overall safety.
