Cookin' Ynah: The Food Fighter

Cookin' Ynah: The Food Fighter is a 2D action game where players control Ynah, a skilled chef who battles against various food-themed enemies in a culinary adventure. The game features fast-paced combat, unique power-ups, and vibrant visuals that bring the kitchen to life. Players can upgrade Ynah's abilities and unlock new recipes as they progress through challenging levels filled with deliciously dangerous foes.

Project Details

Cookin' Ynah: The Food Fighter was developed as a passion project to combine the love of cooking and gaming. The game was designed to provide an entertaining and engaging experience for players of all ages. The development process involved creating unique character designs, designing challenging levels, and implementing smooth gameplay mechanics to ensure that players have a fun and enjoyable time battling against the food-themed enemies.
