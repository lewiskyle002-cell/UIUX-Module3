Helpful Librarian

Helpful Librarian is a mobile application designed to assist users in finding and accessing library resources. The app provides a user-friendly interface that allows users to search for books, check availability, and reserve materials. It also offers features such as personalized recommendations and notifications for due dates.

Project Details

The Helpful Librarian project was developed with the goal of improving the library experience for users. The app was designed to be intuitive and easy to navigate, with a focus on providing quick access to library resources. The project involved extensive research and user testing to ensure that the app meets the needs of library patrons and enhances their overall experience.
